About Chapter One Coffee

Chapter One Coffee is an independent, family-run cafe on Barber Road in Sheffield, earning a 4.8-star Google rating from over 120 reviews.

The team is committed to sourcing locally — bread from Depot Bakery, coffee from Smith Street Coffee Roasters, and all cakes and pastries made by local bakers. With a warm, cosy atmosphere, wheelchair-accessible entrance, and friendly staff, Chapter One has become a beloved neighbourhood spot for breakfast, brunch, and the perfect cup of coffee.
