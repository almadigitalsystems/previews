What We Do

Complete Tree Care Services

Whether you need a hazardous tree removed or your property cleared after a storm, our certified crew handles every job safely and efficiently.

🌲

Tree Removal

Safe, complete removal of dead, diseased, or hazardous trees. We handle trees of all sizes with precision equipment and zero damage to your property.

✂️

Tree Trimming & Pruning

Shape your trees for health, safety, and curb appeal. We remove dead branches, improve structure, and let in more light — all while preserving the tree's natural form.

🪨

Stump Grinding

Eliminate unsightly stumps that attract pests and create mowing hazards. We grind stumps below grade so you can replant, seed, or level the area.

⛈️

Emergency Storm Service

Fallen tree on your roof? Dangerous limbs hanging over your driveway? We're available 24/7 for emergency response throughout the Spokane region.

🔍

Hazard Assessment

Not sure if a tree is safe? Our certified arborists inspect root systems, canopy, and structure to give you a full report — with honest recommendations.

🏡

Land Clearing

Preparing a lot for construction or reclaiming overgrown property? We clear trees, brush, and stumps efficiently, leaving you a clean, usable space.
