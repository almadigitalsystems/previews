What We Do

Complete Roofing Services
for East Ridge Homeowners

From minor repairs to full replacements, we handle every roofing need with precision and care. When Tennessee storms hit, we're the crew East Ridge trusts to show up and get it done right.

Roof Replacement

Full tear-off and replacement with premium shingles. We work with all major manufacturers and stand behind every installation we complete.

Roof Repair

Leaks, missing shingles, damaged flashing — we fix it fast. Most repairs completed same-day or next-day to prevent interior damage from spreading.

Storm Damage Restoration

Hail, wind, and heavy rain wreak havoc on Tennessee roofs. We document damage, work with your insurance company, and restore your roof completely.

Gutters & Drainage

Seamless gutter installation, repair, and cleaning. Proper drainage protects your roof, foundation, and landscaping for years to come.

Free Roof Inspections

Not sure what condition your roof is in? We offer thorough inspections with a detailed report — no obligation, no pressure, just honest information.

Commercial Roofing

Flat roofs, TPO, modified bitumen — we handle commercial properties throughout the Chattanooga area with minimal business disruption.
