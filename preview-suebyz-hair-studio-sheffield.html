Our Story

35 Years of Getting It Right

Suebyz Hair Studio has been a Sheffield institution for over three decades. Located on Shoreham Street in Highfield, we've built a loyal following by focusing on one thing: exceptional cutting technique.

While trends come and go, the fundamentals of great hairdressing remain constant. Precision, patience, and listening to you — that's what sets a truly great haircut apart.

Every client receives a full consultation before any scissors come out. We take the time to understand your hair type, your lifestyle, and what you actually want.
