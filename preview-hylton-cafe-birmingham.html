Our Story

A Jewellery Quarter Staple Since 2016

Hylton Cafe has been a staple of Birmingham's Jewellery Quarter since 2016, welcoming locals, creatives, and workers for hearty breakfasts and proper brunch.

Known for generous portions and a warm, no-fuss atmosphere. Honest food and great coffee — that's what we've always been about. Whether you're a regular or finding us for the first time, you're always welcome at Hylton.
