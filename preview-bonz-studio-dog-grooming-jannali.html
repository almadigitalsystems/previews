About Us

Bonz Studio Dog Grooming is a professional dog grooming salon located in Jannali, in Sydney’s Sutherland Shire. Offering a full range of grooming services from puppy introductions to breed-specific styling, Bonz Studio is known for a calm, patient approach that keeps dogs comfortable and owners happy. Pet parents across the Shire trust Bonz for consistently clean, well-styled results and genuine care for every dog that walks through the door.
