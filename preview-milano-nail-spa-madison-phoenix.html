Why north Phoenix booked Milano Madison the week we opened

Milano started in Houston. The Phoenix location was opened with the same five senior technicians, the same supply lines, and one new addition — a dip formula that the Houston salon doesn't even sell, because Phoenix afternoons demanded it.

Madison isn't a sister-store-on-autopilot. It's a separately-stocked salon for the desert, with its own manager, its own walk-in window, and its own seasonal palette inspired by Camelback at golden hour.
