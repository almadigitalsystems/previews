What We Fix

Expert Repair for All Major Appliances

From your kitchen to your laundry room — Appliance Clinic's certified technicians repair all major brands and appliance types. Most repairs are completed on the first visit.

🫧

Washer & Dryer Repair

Won't spin, won't drain, won't start? We diagnose and fix all washer and dryer problems — top-load, front-load, gas, and electric.

Most Requested
❄️

Refrigerator Repair

Not cooling, leaking water, strange noises, or ice maker issues — we fix all refrigerator brands and styles including French door and side-by-side.

🍽️

Dishwasher Repair

Not cleaning, not draining, not starting, or leaving dishes wet? We'll have your dishwasher back in service fast — often same-day.

🔥

Oven, Range & Stove

Burner won't light, oven won't heat, temperature off? We repair gas and electric ovens, ranges, and cooktops from all major brands.

🌀

Microwave Repair

Over-the-range and countertop microwaves repaired. If it's not heating, making sparks, or not turning on, we can diagnose it fast.

🧊

Freezer Repair

Chest or upright freezers not maintaining temperature, frosting over, or making noise. We restore proper function quickly.
