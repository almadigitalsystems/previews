Methodology

How we sourced this data

Carepriced doesn't take operator advertising and isn't a referral marketplace. Every number on this page is sourced and dated. Here's exactly how the v0 estimate is computed.

v0 — State-anchored estimate methodology
  • State median anchor: Genworth/CareScout Cost of Care Survey 2024, North Carolina SNF private room.
  • Metro tier multipliers: Tier A (Charlotte/Raleigh-Durham/Triad metro) = 1.15× · Tier B (mid-size MSAs) = 1.05× · Tier C (small metro) = 1.00× · Tier D (rural) = 0.92×.
  • Per-facility low/high range: Derived from tier-adjusted state range bands (low = state low × tier, high = state high × tier).
  • Per-record citation: Every operator detail page lists its sources, retrieval date, and any inferences applied.
  • Phase 1-full upgrade: Once we ingest CMS Form 2540-24 cost reports per facility (target Q3 2026), badges flip from "state-anchored estimate" to "per-facility cost report" with filing references.
