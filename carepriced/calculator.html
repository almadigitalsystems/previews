What this means

What we estimated, in plain English

Three things you should know about this number before you trust it.

1

It's a state-anchored estimate

v0 numbers come from Genworth/CareScout 2024 state median + a metro-tier multiplier (A 1.15× / B 1.05× / C 1.00× / D 0.92×). Per-facility cost-report data lands later this year.

2

The range matters more than the median

SNF costs vary ±20–30% within a state based on tier, ownership, and bed type (private vs semi-private). The range we show reflects that real spread.

3

Most families combine programs

Out-of-pocket alone is rare. VA Aid & Attendance, NC Medicaid waivers, and existing LTC policies typically offset 30–60% of the monthly figure.
