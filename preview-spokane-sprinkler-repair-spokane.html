What We Fix & Install

Full-Range Irrigation Services

Whether it's a single broken head or a full system overhaul, we handle jobs of every size.

01

Sprinkler Head Replacement

Broken, sunken, or clogged heads replaced correctly with the right spray pattern for your lawn.

02

Valve Repair & Replacement

Faulty valves cause zones to not run or run constantly. We diagnose and replace damaged or stuck valves.

03

Controller Programming

We program your system for seasonal watering schedules and upgrade to smart WiFi controllers on request.

04

Leak Detection & Line Repair

Underground leaks waste water and kill your lawn. We locate the source accurately and repair it completely.

05

Spring Startup & Inspection

Turn-on, pressure test, head adjustment, and zone review to get your system ready for the growing season.

06

Fall Winterization (Blowout)

Complete compressed-air blowout of all zones before Spokane's first hard freeze — prevents burst pipes.

07

Backflow Preventer Testing

Annual backflow certification required by Spokane Water. Certified testing, completed and filed efficiently.

08

New System Installation

Designing and installing a new irrigation system for a new build or lawn expansion. Zoned and efficient.
