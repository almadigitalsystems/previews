What We Do

Complete Irrigation Services

From designing and installing a brand-new system to fixing a single broken head — we handle every aspect of residential and commercial irrigation in Spokane.

💧

Sprinkler System Installation

Complete irrigation system design and installation for new and existing properties. Zoned coverage, water-efficient heads, and professional-grade components built to last.

🔧

Sprinkler Repair & Service

Broken heads, valve failures, controller issues, line leaks — we diagnose and repair any irrigation problem quickly and correctly, on all major brands.

🌸

Spring System Startup

Turn-on, pressurization, head inspection and adjustment, backflow check, and zone programming to get your system fully operational for spring.

❄️

Fall Winterization

Full compressed-air blowout of every zone before Spokane's first hard freeze. Protects your investment from burst lines and cracked fittings.

⚙️

System Upgrades & Expansions

Upgrade to smart WiFi controllers, add new zones for expanded coverage, or retrofit an older system with modern heads for better efficiency.

📋

Backflow Testing & Certification

Annual backflow preventer testing and certification as required by Spokane Water. Certified testers on staff — we file your paperwork and keep your system compliant.
