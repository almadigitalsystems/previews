A Family Business Built on Craftsmanship

Flores Painting is a family-owned painting company led by Edwin Flores, serving residential and commercial clients across Dallas and the greater DFW metroplex for over 20 years.

Known for meticulous surface preparation, clean lines, and reliable timelines, the Flores team handles everything from single-room refreshes to full exterior repaints. Two decades of consistent quality have made them a trusted name in Dallas home improvement.
