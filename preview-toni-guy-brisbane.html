Our Story

Queensland's Premier Salon for Over 20 Years

TONI&GUY Brisbane has been Queensland's premier salon for over 20 years, led by owners Kimberley and Lyndsay at 150 Edward Street in the Brisbane CBD. We were Queensland's first TONI&GUY salon when we opened in 2003, and we've set the benchmark for professional hair services ever since.

Voted Best Hairdresser by Style Magazine for seven consecutive years, our team combines global TONI&GUY training with deep local expertise to deliver results that consistently exceed expectations.
