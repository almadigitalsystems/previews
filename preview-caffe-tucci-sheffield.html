Our Story

Caffe Tucci is a Neapolitan caffe and deli on Surrey Street in Sheffield, founded by Stefano Brasile. With a 4.9-star Google rating from over 650 reviews, it has become one of Sheffield's most beloved independent eateries.

The menu features authentic Italian focaccia, handmade pastries, and specialty coffee — all served by a passionate team that makes every customer feel at home. Open seven days a week, Caffe Tucci is where Sheffield comes for a genuine taste of Italy.
