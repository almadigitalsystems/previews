Frequently asked

Common EV charger questions

Quick answers to what {{city}} homeowners and businesses ask us most.

How long does a home EV charger install take?
Most home installs are done in 2–4 hours. If your electrical panel needs an upgrade or a long conduit run is required, we'll let you know up front and finish in one visit.
Do I need a panel upgrade?
If your home has a 100A panel built before 2010, there's a good chance you'll need a 200A upgrade to safely add a Level 2 charger. We do a free load calculation as part of every quote so you know before we start.
Can I claim the federal EV charger tax credit?
Yes — eligible homeowners can claim up to 30% of the install cost (max $1,000) under the federal Alternative Fuel Refueling Property Credit. We provide an itemized invoice you can submit with IRS Form 8911.
What's included in your quote?
Every quote includes the breaker, wiring, conduit, mounting hardware, all permits, the city inspection, and our 5-year workmanship warranty. No surprise add-ons.
Are you certified by Tesla / Ford / GM?
Yes — we're a Tesla Certified Installer and have completed Ford Pro Charging and GM Energy installer training. We work with every major OEM charger brand.
