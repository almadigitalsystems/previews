Nearly Eight Decades of Excellence

Kraft Professional Cleaners has served San Antonio since 1947, making them one of the city’s longest-running dry cleaning operations. Now led by Leslie Staas, Kraft combines decades of fabric care expertise with modern cleaning technology. From everyday business attire to delicate heirloom garments, every piece gets the careful, expert treatment that built Kraft’s reputation over nearly eight decades.
