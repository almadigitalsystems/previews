Our Story

35+ Years of Honest Service & Community Trust

Wade Hatchell Heating & Cooling, Inc. was founded in 1989 with one simple principle: treat every customer the way you'd want your own family treated. What started as a local one-man operation has grown into one of Central Kentucky's most respected HVAC companies — built entirely on referrals, reputation, and results.

As a Carrier Authorized Dealer, we install and service some of the most reliable HVAC equipment available. That means you get industry-leading products backed by the expertise of a team that has been working in this community for over three decades.

We serve homeowners and businesses throughout Lawrenceburg, Frankfort, and the surrounding Anderson County communities. Whether it's a mid-summer emergency or a routine furnace tune-up before the first frost, we show up on time and get the job done right.
