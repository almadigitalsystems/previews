Three colorists, one room, no shortcuts

Goa was started by three colorists who wanted a chair that didn't have a corporate parent. Bloor West gave us the brick wall and the morning light; the rest came from books we had already built.

We don't sell memberships. We don't push smoothing on every client. The consult is honest — sometimes a few highlights are the right answer, and sometimes the answer is a trim and a Sunday off.

The redesign you're looking at is the same room, the same chairs, the same conversation — just in a homepage that actually shows it.
