Our Story

A Boutique Studio
Built for You

Bohemy Hair Co is a boutique eco-luxe salon tucked into Sheffield's creative Kelham Island neighbourhood — a space designed to feel as good as the colour you leave with. We're ethical enthusiasts who believe stunning results and sustainable practice go hand in hand.

Rated 4.9 stars by our Sheffield community, we specialise in balayage and bespoke colour work, served with a luxury drinks menu and zero rush. Every appointment is unhurried, intentional, and entirely yours.
