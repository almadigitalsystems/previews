Southern Wisconsin's Trusted Masonry Specialists

At Pioneer Masonry Restoration, we believe that every brick, stone, and mortar joint tells a story worth preserving. Based in Evansville, WI, we've dedicated ourselves to the art and science of masonry restoration — from ancient chimney stacks to century-old foundation walls.

Owner Nathan Gaustad brings a meticulous eye for historical detail and a commitment to materials that match the originals. Whether it's grinding out failed mortar joints and packing fresh tuck pointing, or sourcing matching brick for seamless replacement, we treat every project as if it were our own home.

We serve Evansville, Janesville, Whitewater, Milton, Edgerton, and communities throughout Rock and Walworth counties.

Our Masonry Services

Comprehensive restoration and installation for residential and commercial properties throughout Southern Wisconsin

Chimney & Masonry Facade Restoration

Full chimney rebuilds, crown repair, flashing replacement, and facade repointing to protect your home from water intrusion and structural damage.

Tuck Pointing & Brick Replacement

Precision mortar joint removal and repacking to stop water infiltration. We source matching brick and stone to make repairs invisible.

Stone Patios & Retaining Walls

Natural and manufactured stone patio installation, retaining walls, garden borders, and landscape masonry built to last decades.

Foundation & Basement Wall Repair

Crack injection, block wall rebuilding, and masonry resurfacing to restore structural integrity and prevent water intrusion in foundations.

Historical Restoration

Sensitive restoration of historic structures using period-appropriate materials and techniques that meet preservation standards.

Resurfacing & Aggregate Blasting

Surface preparation, masonry resurfacing, and aggregate blasting to clean, restore, and renew aging masonry surfaces.
