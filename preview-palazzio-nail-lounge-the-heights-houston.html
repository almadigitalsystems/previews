Why The Heights chose Studemont Street

When the River Oaks lounge filled up, Palazzio opened a second location — but only after finding a building with the right windows and the right ceiling height. Studemont gave us both.

The studio was designed to be the opposite of a salon you have to talk over. Soft music, six chairs spaced for privacy, technicians who don't push add-ons. Our regulars come because the room itself does most of the convincing.

There's no membership program. There never will be. Show up, get the work done well, and book the next visit on your way out — the way it's supposed to be.
