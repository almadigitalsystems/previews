About L1 Nails

Peyton's Place on Lord Street

L1 Nails — known to regulars as Peyton's Place — has become one of Liverpool city centre's most loved nail studios, tucked right on Lord Street in the L1 postcode.

Owner Peyton has built a loyal following through precision, creativity, and a warm atmosphere that keeps clients coming back. Every set is treated as a showcase, whether it's a clean nude gel or an intricate design that turns heads.

No walk-in chaos, no shortcuts. Just proper nail care done right in the heart of Liverpool.
