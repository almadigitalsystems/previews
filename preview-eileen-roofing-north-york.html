What We Do

Roofing Services in North York

From routine maintenance to full system replacements, we handle every aspect of residential and commercial roofing.

🏠

Asphalt Shingle Roofing

Complete installation and replacement of asphalt shingles. We carry all major brands with manufacturer warranties.

🔧

Roof Repairs & Patching

Cracked flashing, missing shingles, ice dam damage. Our crew identifies the root cause and fixes it properly.

🌧

Leak Detection & Waterproofing

We trace leaks to their exact source and apply professional-grade waterproofing membranes for lasting protection.

🏢

Flat & Low-Slope Roofing

EPDM, TPO, and modified bitumen systems for commercial and residential flat roofs. Installed to drain, seal, and last.

Complete exterior trim and drainage system work. We replace rotting soffits and install seamless eavestroughs.

Roof Inspections & Reports

Pre-purchase, insurance, or annual inspections with a detailed written report for Toronto homeowners.
