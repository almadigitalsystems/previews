What We Repair

Every Repair, Done Right

From a quick heel replacement to a full sole rebuild — Alex and the Old World Cobbler team handles every repair with the same level of care.

👞

Sole & Heel Replacement

Worn-down heels and separated soles repaired with quality rubber and leather. Extends shoe life by years. Works on dress shoes, boots, and casual footwear.

Most Common
🥾

Boot Resoling & Rebuild

Vibram sole replacements, full boot rebuilds, and Goodyear welt recrafting. We restore work boots, hiking boots, and fashion boots to factory condition.

Specialty
🎨

Leather Dyeing & Refinishing

Restore faded, scuffed, or discolored leather shoes and goods. Color match, color change, and protective coating applied by hand for a lasting finish.

👜

Bag & Purse Repair

Broken handles, torn seams, worn corners, broken zippers, and hardware replacement. Designer handbag and leather bag repair — all brands accepted.

🔧

Stitching & Seam Repair

Delaminating soles, torn stitching, and leather seam failures repaired with professional-grade thread and adhesives. Built to last, not just to look good.

✨

Shoe Stretching & Conditioning

Tight shoes stretched to custom fit. Full deep conditioning treatments restore suppleness and protect leather from cracking. Your shoes will feel brand new.
