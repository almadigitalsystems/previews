About Us

Seasonal Services

We're With You Year-Round

Spokane's weather demands a seasonal irrigation strategy. We've got every part of the calendar covered.

🌸 Spring Startup (April – May)

  • System pressurization and leak inspection
  • Head adjustment and coverage testing for each zone
  • Controller programming for seasonal watering schedule
  • Backflow preventer activation and certification
  • Repair of any winter damage found during startup

🌿 Summer Maintenance (June – August)

  • Mid-season system checkups to catch dry spots early
  • Controller adjustments for weather and water restrictions
  • Emergency repairs for broken heads and burst lines
  • Water efficiency audits to reduce your water bill

❄️ Fall Winterization (Oct – Nov)

  • Complete blow-out of all zones with commercial compressor
  • Valve shutdown and controller winterization
  • Documentation of any issues found during shutdown
  • Priority spring scheduling for returning clients

🔧 Repair & Upgrades (Year-Round)

  • Broken or clogged heads — any brand, any age
  • Valve and solenoid replacement
  • Controller upgrades (smart/WiFi controllers available)
  • New zone additions for expanded coverage
