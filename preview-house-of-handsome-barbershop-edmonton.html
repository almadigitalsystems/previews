About House of Handsome

Where Classic Craft Meets Modern Comfort

House of Handsome is Edmonton’s premier barbershop, with locations across Cameron Heights, Whyte Ave, and beyond. Known for master barbers, signature hot towel shaves, and an atmosphere that blends classic craft with modern comfort — dark wood panels, plush leather chairs, ambient lighting.

They’ve built a loyal community one sharp cut at a time. Voted Edmonton’s best barbershop, they’re open 7 days a week so you can always walk in looking your best.

  • Multiple Edmonton locations — Cameron Heights, Whyte Ave & more
  • Voted Edmonton’s best barbershop
  • Open 7 days a week — always accessible
  • Master barbers — not apprentices
  • Classic atmosphere with dark wood, leather & ambient lighting
