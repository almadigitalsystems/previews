Our Story

Born From Passion. Built on Loyalty.

Founded in 2014 by three passionate Miami stylists, Saints-N-Scissors is North Miami's full-service salon and body art destination.

Known for their expertise in curly, long, and damaged hair, they've built a loyal following on personalized service and a vibe that's equal parts edgy and welcoming.

Find them at 11091 Biscayne Blvd, Suite B — where rebels get glam and everyone leaves looking exactly like themselves, only better.
