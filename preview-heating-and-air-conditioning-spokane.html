Our Services

HVAC Services for Every Season

Heating, cooling, and air quality solutions tailored to Spokane's climate — hot summers and cold winters mean your HVAC system works hard year-round.

Heating Systems

Furnace and heat pump installations, repairs, and maintenance. Keep your Spokane home warm through the coldest winters with an efficient, properly sized heating system.

Air Conditioning

Central AC, ductless mini-splits, and cooling system repairs. Stay cool and comfortable during Spokane's summer heat without sky-high energy bills.

Preventive Maintenance

Annual HVAC tune-ups that extend equipment life, improve efficiency, and prevent costly emergency breakdowns. Schedule your service before the season starts.

Indoor Air Quality

Air purifiers, humidifiers, and ventilation upgrades. Breathe cleaner, healthier air in your home — especially important during Spokane's wildfire smoke seasons.

System Replacements

Old HVAC system reaching the end of its life? We'll help you choose the right replacement for your home's size, budget, and efficiency goals — then install it right.

Emergency Service

HVAC breakdowns don't wait for business hours. When you need urgent heating or cooling help, we respond fast to get your system running again.
