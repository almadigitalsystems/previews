The Gallery

A Meeting Place for Discerning Collectors

Founded in 2015 at the entrance of Place Royale in Old Quebec, Galerie Douce Passion is the meeting place for discerning collectors who appreciate the masters of Quebec and Canadian painting. The gallery features works by renowned artists including Bruno Côté, René Richard, Marc-Aurèle Fortin, and Jean-Paul Lemieux, alongside exciting contemporary talent. Whether you visit in person at 42A rue Notre-Dame or browse online, every piece is selected with the same passion for excellence.
